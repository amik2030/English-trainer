🏗️ Architecture

Three-Layer Design

Each layer has a clear role. Together, they create a learning system that scales without losing the human touch.

🎛️

Teacher Dashboard

The Control Tower
📚

Curriculum Builder

Design conversation topics, vocabulary lists, and difficulty levels aligned to your syllabus. Map content to specific chapters and learning objectives.

💬

Prompt Templates

Define the AI tutor's behavior: tone, correction style, topics to avoid, cultural sensitivity rules. Your voice, amplified by AI.

✅

Approval Gates

Require certain conversations to be reviewed before students progress. Like signing off on homework — you stay in control of pacing.

🚩

Flag Review Queue

AI automatically flags conversations where students struggled, used inappropriate language, or need intervention. Focus your attention where it matters.

🤖

AI Tutor

The Practice Partner
🎯

Boundary-Aware

Operates strictly within teacher-set parameters. Cannot go off-topic beyond allowances. Follows approved curriculum and vocabulary.

📝

Full Logging

Every conversation is recorded for teacher review. Complete transparency into what's happening in each session.

🔄

Adaptive Practice

Adjusts difficulty based on student performance within the teacher-defined framework. Challenges without overwhelming.

⏰

Always Available

24/7 practice partner. Students can practice anytime — the AI is patient, consistent, and never tired.

📱

Student App

The Practice Ground
🎙️

Voice Conversation

Real spoken practice with AI. Record your voice, hear responses, build confidence in actual speaking — not just reading.

📊

Progress Tracking

See your accuracy, session time, word count, and conversation turns. Visible progress motivates continued practice.

🎓

Teacher-Assigned Content

Practice what your teacher assigned — not generic topics. Every session connects to your learning path.

🏆

30-Day Challenge

Structured daily practice builds habits. 15 minutes a day, 30 days, 3x faster progress than traditional methods.

🛡️ Safety First

Guardrails & Protection

Every layer includes safeguards to protect students and give teachers full visibility.

🔒

Content Filtering

Teacher-defined appropriate topics. AI cannot go outside approved boundaries.

👁️

Conversation Monitoring

All interactions logged and reviewable. Teachers see everything that happens.

🚨

Escalation Triggers

Automatic flags for bullying, self-harm indicators, or concerning language.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Visibility

Dashboard for parents to see their child's progress and conversation summaries.

🎂

Age-Appropriate

AI responses calibrated for age group. Younger students get simpler, safer interactions.

🚫

No PII Leakage

Personal information never shared with AI models or stored insecurely.

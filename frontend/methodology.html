🌱 Human-in-the-Loop

Teachers Don't Get Replaced.
They Become Conductors.

Our methodology puts teachers at the center of AI-powered learning. Teachers design the curriculum, set the guardrails, and intervene when it matters. AI handles the repetitive practice; humans handle the high-value moments.

💡 Our Core Idea

The Human-in-the-Loop Approach

Research shows AI + human guidance produces significantly better outcomes than AI alone. We don't just add a teacher dashboard — we create a continuous feedback loop where the human is always in the middle, shaping what the AI does.

Teacher's Voice in Every Interaction

The teacher's personality, tone, and cultural rules are baked into the AI's behavior. It's not a generic chatbot — it's an extension of the teacher.

🧠

Student Mental Models

Teachers write student profiles — strengths, weaknesses, personality, goals. The AI uses this to personalize every interaction, and the teacher refines it over time.

🔄

Living Curriculum

The curriculum evolves. When a teacher reviews a conversation and adjusts a prompt, the next session is better. The system learns from the human, not just the student.

👩‍🏫 The Teacher's Role

Amplified, Not Replaced

AI handles the drudgery. Teachers focus on what only humans can do: inspire, mentor, and connect.

🤖 What AI Does

  • 1-on-1 conversation practice
  • Grammar correction & feedback
  • Progress tracking & analytics
  • Repetitive drills & exercises
  • 24/7 availability
  • Consistent, patient responses
  • Immediate scoring

👩‍🏫 What Teachers Do

  • Design curriculum & learning paths
  • Review flagged conversations

  • Creative & cultural coaching
  • Emotional support & motivation
  • Context-specific guidance
  • High-value human connection

Four Levels of Human Involvement

Teachers choose how deeply they want to be involved. Every level keeps the human in control.

1

Human Designs, AI Delivers

Teacher creates the curriculum, AI tutors execute it. The teacher is the architect of every learning path.

2

Human Monitors, AI Adapts

AI runs the session, but the teacher watches live and can intervene — like a supervisor in a call center.

3

Human Co-Teaches with AI

Teacher and AI tag-team. AI handles drill and practice; teacher handles creative, emotional, and cultural moments.

4

Human Reviews After

AI runs everything; teacher reviews transcripts asynchronously and gives feedback when it matters most.

🎓 Value for Learners

Why This Partnership Works

When teachers and AI work together, learners get the best of both worlds: the consistency and patience of AI, combined with the wisdom and care of a human educator.

🎯

Personalized Learning Paths

Every conversation is tailored to the student's level, goals, and personality — as designed by their teacher. Not generic practice, but targeted skill-building.

🔒

Safe Learning Environment

AI removes the fear of judgment. Students practice freely, making mistakes without embarrassment. Teachers monitor and intervene only when needed.

⚡

Immediate + Expert Feedback

AI provides instant grammar correction and suggestions. Teachers add deeper insights, cultural context, and encouragement that only a human can give.

📈

Visible Progress

Students see their accuracy, word count, and session time improve. Teachers track progress across their class and identify who needs extra support.

🌍

Culturally Relevant Content

Teachers ensure conversations respect cultural norms and use locally relevant scenarios. Practice ordering food at the canteen, not a Parisian café.

🏆

Confidence Building

30 days of daily practice with patient AI builds speaking confidence. Teacher encouragement and recognition accelerate motivation and commitment.

🛡️ Safety First

Guardrails & Protection

Every layer includes safeguards to protect students and give teachers full visibility.

🔒

Content Filtering

Teacher-defined appropriate topics. AI cannot go outside approved boundaries.

👁️

Conversation Monitoring

All interactions logged and reviewable. Teachers see everything that happens.

🚨

Escalation Triggers

Automatic flags for bullying, self-harm indicators, or concerning language.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Visibility

Dashboard for parents to see their child's progress and conversation summaries.

🎂

Age-Appropriate

AI responses calibrated for age group. Younger students get simpler, safer interactions.

🚫

No PII Leakage

Personal information never shared with AI models or stored insecurely.
